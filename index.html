Woof Gym is an interactive voice-recognition webzine where my dog Aki becomes the trainer.

Visitors join Aki’s workout class and activate each exercise by speaking commands. The routine begins with a warm-up and continues through a sequence of movements—including bow, figure eight, sit, plank, and rest.

As the audience speaks, the system listens and responds with images and text, allowing Aki to guide everyone through a playful canine training session.
